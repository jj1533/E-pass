Rules and Regulations

  • The interstate medical permit is only a one way permit to exit the state.

  • Strict actions shall be taken against those found misusing the E-Pass.

  • The pass holders are requested to carry the driving license.

  • The state authority is liable to cancel the pass anytime.

  • Persons on Government/Medical/Press Media should carry their respective ID Cards.

  • For interstate permits may kindly apply to respective District Magistrates of the district you are residing in.

  • The vehicle E-Pass is only for interstate travel within the state of Maharashtra and to exit the state.

  • This vehicle E-Pass is non transferrable.
